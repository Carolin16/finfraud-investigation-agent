ACCOUNTS PAYABLE POLICY — PHANTOM DELIVERY AND GOODS RECEIPT VALIDATION
Document Reference: AP-POL-003
Issued by: Finance Compliance, Internal Controls Division
Effective Date: January 2024 | Review Date: January 2025

1. PURPOSE
This policy establishes mandatory goods receipt validation procedures before
any invoice payment is released. Phantom delivery fraud — where vendors
invoice for goods or services never delivered — is one of the most damaging
forms of AP fraud.

2. THREE-WAY MATCH REQUIREMENT (GOODS RECEIPT FOCUS)
No invoice may be approved for payment unless the Goods Receipt (GR) amount
is confirmed and reconciled against the invoice amount within acceptable
tolerance. A GR amount of zero constitutes an automatic payment block.

3. ZERO GR POLICY
If the system records a Goods Receipt amount of $0.00 at the time of invoice
submission, the invoice is automatically blocked. The block may not be
released by any AP staff member without physical confirmation from the
receiving warehouse team.

4. WAREHOUSE CONFIRMATION REQUIREMENTS
The AP team must obtain written confirmation from the Warehouse Manager or
designated site lead confirming:
  — The goods or services described in the invoice were received
  — The quantity and condition of goods matches the invoice
  — The date of receipt

Verbal confirmation is not acceptable. Confirmation must be documented in
the AP system within 5 business days.

5. VENDOR OBLIGATIONS
Vendors must be able to supply the following on request:
  — Signed proof of delivery (POD)
  — Carrier tracking number and delivery confirmation
  — Photos or inspection records where applicable

Failure to provide proof of delivery within 10 business days results in
automatic invoice rejection.

6. ESCALATION
Suspected phantom delivery cases must be escalated to the Head of Compliance
within 24 hours of confirmation that no goods were received.
Repeated phantom delivery attempts by a vendor result in immediate suspension
from the approved vendor list.

7. RECORD RETENTION
All GR validation records, warehouse confirmations, and vendor
communications must be retained for 7 years.
