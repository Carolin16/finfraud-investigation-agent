1. CONTRACTED RATE
The contracted rate for MedCo Supplies Inc is set within the
following band:

  Minimum rate  : $10,800.00 per transaction/shipment
  Typical rate  : $12,000.00 per transaction/shipment
  Maximum rate  : $13,200.00 per transaction/shipment

These rates reflect the agreed pricing for standard medical
goods and services as specified in the master vendor agreement.

2. VARIANCE CLAUSE
Invoices may deviate from the contracted typical rate by a maximum of
5% without requiring additional approvals, subject to the
three-way match passing.

Any invoice exceeding the contracted maximum rate of
$13,200.00 requires written justification from the vendor
and approval from Emma Thornton, Head of Procurement before payment is released.

Rate changes above the maximum contracted rate must be agreed in writing
at least 30 days before the new rate invoice is submitted.

3. PAYMENT TERMS
Payment terms: Net 30 from confirmed Goods Receipt date.
Late payment may attract a penalty of 2% per month on overdue amounts.

4. HIGH-VALUE INVOICE THRESHOLD
Invoices above $13,800 require escalated approval:
  — Emma Thornton, Head of Procurement
  — Head of Finance sign-off

5. THREE-WAY MATCH REQUIREMENT
All invoices from MedCo Supplies Inc must pass a three-way match
between invoice amount, PO amount, and Goods Receipt amount before
payment is released. A zero Goods Receipt will result in automatic
payment block.

6. ANNUAL RATE REVIEW
Contract rates are reviewed annually each October. Rate change
proposals must be submitted by the vendor at least 60 days before the
review date.

7. VENDOR NOTES
Medical consumables. Three-way match mandatory on all invoices.

8. CONTRACT STATUS
Active — in good standing as of the date of this summary.
